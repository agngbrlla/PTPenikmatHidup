Our Product

Rice is generally produced by milling and separating the bran from the rice grains. The rice products that we produce are agricultural products of local farmers in Indonesia or more precisely the province of South Sulawesi which is well known for its good agricultural products.

We source bran products from local farmers in South Sulawesi, Indonesia, who are known for their good agricultural quality, high quality which helps in maintaining digestive health, increasing productivity, and improving the quality of livestock rations.
